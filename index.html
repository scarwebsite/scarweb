What Is

SCARCITY TOKEN (SCAR)?

SCAR Token is a new ultra-deflationary, super low supply social experiment coin. When coins are sent, 6% of every transaction is burnt. Combined with a very low 200 token initial supply, SCAR tokens will become extremely rare and scarce (this is what the name Scarcity Token is based on). SCAR was created as a social experiment to see how holders behave when given a very low supply and ultra deflationary token. 100% of the SCAR supply will be airdropped to the crypto community to allow the experiment to play out as organically as possible. As a community driven coin, eventually control of the project will be handed to the community to manage and lead.
